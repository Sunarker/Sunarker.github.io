Jiangchao Yao

EE, SJTU, Sunarker@sjtu.edu.cn

I am a dual-degree PhD candidate of Electronic Engineering at Shanghai Jiao Tong University (Supervised by Prof. Ya Zhang) and University of Technology Sydney (Supervised by Prof. Ivor W. Tsang). I work in the field of social media analysis with machine learning techniques.

My research interests include:

  • Web mining on text and images
  • Probabilistic graphical models
  • Deep representation learning

Recently, I am working on representation learning on noisy graph.
